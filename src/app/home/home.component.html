The School

Lewin School is a community-based institution in rural Kenya. The school's founding was inspired by an increase in school dropout rates mainly due to cultural practices inconsistent with the idea of education.

The Objectives

The funding will enable the school to model a transormational and community-inclusive educational system. This will:

  1. Create opportunities for students
  2. Propel the shift of social hierarchy
  3. Roll out mutually beneficial student and community-focused programs
This will result in reduced levels of poverty and increased access to quality education.
